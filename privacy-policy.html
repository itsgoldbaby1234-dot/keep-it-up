Privacy Policy — Keep it UP!

Last updated: July 3rd 2026

Keep it UP! ("the game," "we," "us") is a free browser-based game. This page explains what data is collected when you play.

Local score data

Your bounce-count scores and the dates you achieved them are stored only in your device's local browser storage, so we can show your top 3 leaderboard. This data never leaves your device and is not sent to us or any third party.

Advertising

This game displays ads served by Google AdSense/AdMob. Google may use cookies and device identifiers to serve ads, including personalized ads based on your activity on this and other sites/apps, unless you have opted out. Google's use of advertising cookies enables it and its partners to serve ads based on your visits to this site and other sites on the Internet.

You can learn more about how Google uses data and manage your ad personalization settings at: policies.google.com/technologies/partner-sites and adssettings.google.com.

No account, no personal data collection by us

This game does not require sign-up or login, and we do not directly collect names, emails, or other personal information.

Children's privacy

This game is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Contact

Questions about this policy can be sent to us using the submission form on the contact page.
