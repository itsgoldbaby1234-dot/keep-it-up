About Keep it UP!

Keep it UP! is a simple, one-touch arcade game: keep a ball bouncing in the air by tapping it before it falls onto the spikes below. It's built to be quick to pick up, hard to put down.

How to play

  • Tap the ball to bounce it upward — the exact angle is a little random each time, so stay sharp.
  • Avoid letting the ball touch the spikes at the bottom of the screen.
  • Rain falls from time to time, making the ball rise slower and fall faster.
  • Wind gusts push the ball left or right — watch the on-screen arrow for direction.
  • Your bounce count is your score. Try to land a spot on the top 3 leaderboard.

About this project

This game was built as a lightweight, installable web app (PWA) — no app-store download required to play in a browser, though it's also available as an installable app. Scores are stored locally on your own device.
